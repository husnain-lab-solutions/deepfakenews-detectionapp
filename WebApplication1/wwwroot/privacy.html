Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Overview

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control your information.

What we collect

  • Account information: email, hashed credentials, and timestamps.
  • Prediction inputs: text snippets or image metadata (file names, sizes). Uploaded images are processed transiently unless you save history.
  • Prediction results: labels, confidence, and timestamps stored in your private history.
  • Basic logs and diagnostics to keep the service reliable.

How we use data

  • Provide predictions and show your private history.
  • Improve detection quality and stability in aggregate, without selling data.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and meet legal requirements.

Your controls

  • Export or delete your prediction history at any time.
  • Request correction or removal of your account information.
  • Opt out of non-essential analytics if enabled in the future.

Data retention

We retain account data while your account is active. Prediction history is stored until you delete it. Transient files are removed after processing unless explicitly saved.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us.
